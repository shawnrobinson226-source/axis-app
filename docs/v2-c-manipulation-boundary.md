# V2-C Manipulation Boundary

## Allowed
- Explicit classification.
- Explicit protocol and step outputs.
- Explicit suggested next actions.
- Visible logging and continuity tracking.
- Operator-controlled invocation and use.

## Forbidden
- Hidden steering.
- Covert output rewriting.
- Undeclared behavior shaping.
- Secret adaptation based on psychological leverage.
- Emotional dependency design.
- Opaque persuasion goals (compliance, retention, submission).
- Identity substitution.
- Invisible optimization layers that alter intent without disclosure.

## Operating Rules
- The user remains the decision authority.
- Reasoning must remain visible through explicit structure and outputs.
- Adapters may not turn AXIS into covert steering middleware.
- Any more directive mode must be explicit, user-invoked, and bounded.
